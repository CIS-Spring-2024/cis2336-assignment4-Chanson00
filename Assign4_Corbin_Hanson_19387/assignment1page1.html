Corbin Hanson
Corbin Hanson is the founder and owner of Houston Happenings, located in the Post Oak area in the Galleria. Mr. Hanson has over 10 years of experience working in restaurants around the city of Houston. Mr. Hanson is very passionate about sampling different types of food and giving up-and-coming chefs a chance to show their talents while creating a modern dining experience with a splash of Houstonian touches paying homage to the sports teams and culture of Houston.

Mr. Hanson began as a cook from the young age of 17 and eventually ventured out into serving guests and moving up the ladder of various restaurants. As Mr. Hanson gained experience and connections, he gained his passion for opening up his very own restaurant.

While running his restaurant, Mr. Hanson is an avid sports fan and attends various houston sports events. This is a big influence on the decorum of the restaurant. With Mr. Hanson being so involved with the city, he uses this to network with the Houston teams to sponsor various events and charity opportunities within the city.

During Mr. Hanson's free time, he likes to attend sporting events, watch new movies and network with various restaurants and organizations throughout the city. This gives him the opportunity to adapt his restaurant to differents tastes and keep his restaurant fresh and trendy.
